About Us

ConfPlus is an online platform that provides users with a comprehensive schedule of annual conferences and events. Our mission is to connect individuals, businesses, and organizations with the latest industry insights and networking opportunities. With our easy-to-use platform, users can browse upcoming events, learn about keynote speakers and session topics, and register for events with just a few clicks. Our annual schedule is constantly updated to provide the latest information and ensure that our users have access to the most valuable events in their field.
